Distribution grids and digital visibility: why metering now matters more than ever

As electrification expands, local distribution grids are asked to perform far beyond historical operating assumptions. That shift requires deep visibility into load behavior and grid states.

Smart metering is not just a billing upgrade; it is a planning and operations layer that supports:

  • More accurate hosting capacity assessments.
  • Time-sensitive flexibility products and dynamic tariffs.
  • Faster diagnosis of congestion and service-quality issues.

Without reliable data granularity, investment and policy decisions are made with avoidable uncertainty. In that sense, digital visibility is now strategic infrastructure.
